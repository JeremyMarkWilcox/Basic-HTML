Below are two examples of my favorite and I believe most practial Okinawan Weapons to learn to defend oneself.

Sai

A sai is an Okinawan weapon (Usually used in pairs) that appears to be for stabbing/thrust attacks but in actuality the instrument was used to blunt and disarm katanas wielded by the invading Japanese samurai. The Okinawan used what they could, modified farmer tools.

Bow(Staff)

A bow(staff) is a Okinawan weapon that is a very efficient weapon for fighting at a distance, as well as quick flailing attacks. This is a useful weapon to learn since many tools and items can be used in place of the traditional wooden form.
